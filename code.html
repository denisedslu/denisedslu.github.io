    Simple library of personal essays from North by Northwestern's graduating class of 2014.

  • World Cup bars in D.C.

    Mapping the best places to watch specific World Cup teams in the D.C. area.

  • Mapping the Purple Line

    Interactive map of the proposed Purple Line in Maryland.

  • Washington Post Date Lab widget

    Spreadsheet-powered widget for WP Magazine's weekly Date Lab feature.

  • Scale of the search for MH370

    Putting into perspective the scale of the search for the missing Malaysia Airline flight 370 using a series of static images. Sometimes the most effective graphics are the simplest.

  • Crimea situation maps

    Some maps of the Ukraine-Russian crisis in Crimea. I worked with PostGraphic's cartographers to design a web-friendly experience of static maps.

  • House of Cards quiz

    Interactive quiz geolocating the locations of the opening sequence of House of Cards. I fooled around with the Google Maps API and tried to not make this too much like GeoGuessr.

  • Mapping D.C.'s historic houses

    D.C. has 160+ historic houses and I mapped them all and had fun with Mapbox and Leaflet documentation.

  • Evolving winter sports

    A history of participants in the sports and individual events of the Winter Olympics. I scraped and manipulated the data with Google Refine and tried to find a way to display all the data within the 60,000+ rows in my various spreadsheets.

  • NBN Year in Media 2013

    NorthbyNorthwestern.com's annual Year in Media project is an interactive that recaps the year's biggest headlines. Taking inspiration from The Verge 50, I planned the concept with NBN's Editor-in-Chief then designed the graphics and coded the project.
